AI-assisted social content for Dubai real estate

Planneri helps time‑constrained marketers and agents generate on‑brand Instagram posts, reels, and LinkedIn updates that comply with local guidelines — fast.

Why Planneri

  • Lead Generation: Goal‑based copy and CTAs to convert viewers to inquiries.
  • Brand & Prestige: Consistent tone and simple branding overlays (logo + color).
  • Time Savings: Wizard streamlines captions, hashtags, and reel voiceover scripts.
  • Compliance‑aware: Heuristic flags for risky claims (mocked in this MVP).

What you can create

  • Instagram Post: Square preview with caption, hashtags, and PNG export.
  • Instagram Reel (concept): Caption plus a generated voiceover script and shot list.
  • LinkedIn Post: Professional headline and paragraph with concise hashtags.
